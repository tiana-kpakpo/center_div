About Me

Ever since birth Christiana Adorkor Kpakpo never allowed anyone to tell her a path was for men and the other was for women. Christiana defines her own path. She is currently transitioning from a man dominated field (Telecommunication Engineering) to another (Software Engineering) She is a proud advocate for women in technology, empower the next generation, and drive social change through tech-driven solutions. Christiana received her Barchelor in Science degree in Telecommunication Engineering from Ghana Communication Technology University.


A detailed-oriented, responsible, and committed engineer, Christiana has designed, implemented, and tested complex web, mobile, virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and machine learning applications using a variety of programming languages and technologies. Christiana is also a quick learner with strong problem-solving, leadership, management, and communication skills, comfortable working as a team or individually.


During her leisure hours, Christiana generously donates her time to a distinguished astronomy club in Ghana. This organization is wholeheartedly committed to advancing the field of astronomy within the country by means of a noble mission: imparting fundamental astronomical knowledge to primary school students. In her role, Christiana engages as a volunteer, ardently sharing her passion for the cosmos and instilling young minds with a deep appreciation for the wonders of astronomy. Through her dedicated efforts, she plays a pivotal role in nurturing the curiosity and understanding of the next generation, thereby fostering a lasting impact on the realm of education and science outreach.
